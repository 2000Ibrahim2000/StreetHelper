I am Alaa Abbasi, a Palestinian street photographer living in Lebanon. Photography has always had a special place in my heart because it allows me to capture the beauty of the hearts and souls of others. Even the simplest image holds a unique story. It provides an escape from reality and allows me to creatively focus on a specific subject matter, highlighting every movement and emotion.

I began my journey as a photographer in 2022 and have been blessed with a lot of love and support from others. When I first started sharing my work on social media, my goal was to bring joy to others and show the beauty of Lebanon.

The Lebanese economic crisis has devasted our community, so I hope to use my platform as a street photographer to spread positivity and bring a smile to the faces of people in need.
